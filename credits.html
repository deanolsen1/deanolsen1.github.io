Project Credits

Book

Thomas Clerc’s book Paris, musée du XXIe siècle: Le dixième arrondissement was published in 2007 by L’Arbalète Gallimard, which holds the copyright. It is also available online through Amazon France.

Text and Translations

Joshua Armstrong conceived this project and also provided the text and translations. Dr. Armstrong is an assistant professor in the Department of French & Italian at the University of Wisconsin­‒Madison.

Site and Map Design

This project was designed and built in the fall of 2014 by Adam Gile, Dean Olsen, and Sijia (Scarlett) Zhang to fulfill requirements of Geography 575: “Interactive Cartography & Geovisualization,” a course offered at the University of Wisconsin‒Madison in its Department of Geography. The course was taught by Dr. Robert Roth and assisted by Carl Sack, a Ph.D. candidate in the department.

Base Map

The underlying base map’s copyright is held by Mapbox and Open Street Maps.

Images

The images used are details from the cover photo of Clerc’s book. We do not know whom to credit.

Icons

The “VCR” icons are made by Freepick.

Languages and Software

This project was written using the resources of Javascript, JQuery, Leaflet, HTML, and CSS. Among the software packages employed in its creation were Mapbox and Adobe’s Photoshop CC, Illustrator CC, and Dreamweaver CC. In addition, Sublime Text 2, Notepad++, and Prepros were invaluable tools for the team.
